We make people genuinely happy

University Clutural And technical Fest

Elements Culmyca, the annual inter-college cul-tech festival of JC Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA has grown on to become one of the most popular college festivals of NCR with 1000+ participating students, 40+ participating colleges and an expected footfall of over 10000 which tends to increase each year.

Protecting trees with Tree Gaurds

Our University believes in Green Campus. DSW has taken a major step towards it by allotting each club a plant. The members of the club have planted their particular plant in the name of their club. Different plant for every club planted in a queue looks beautiful and nature friendly.

Introducing Students to College Life

Induction is a 20 to 30 days continuous event which takes place during the arrival of a new batch so as to introduce them to the University and its clubs. Induction programme gives an opportunity to the newbies to explore the University campus, get to know about the clubs prevailing in the University and also motivates the students by calling renowned public speakers from all over the world.

Fun Night For New Students

Reizo is the name given to the fresher's of JC Bose University, YMCA. It is a small introduction to the clubs of the University as each club presents their performances like dancing, singing, yoga, anchoring and much more. After the fresher's all the clubs start taking their auditions and let the newbies explore their areas of interest and giving them the chance to be a part of the talented groups.

IEEE YMCA SB

Who Are We?

IEEE YMCA SB is a student branch of renowned International technical and professional organisation IEEE. The YMCA Student Branch was founded in 2016 with the objective of providing students, the exposure and knowledge about various technologies and opportunities to work, learn and grow. Its main target is to try and test the new technologies and improve them. We had completed many projects so far and took part in various events and competitions at state and national levels. Some of them are hackathons, ideathons and robotics competitions organized by IEEE branch of other institutions.

Our Work

Solving real life problems in an innovative manner, is what that makes us real engineers. With this goal, IEEE YMCA SB always intends to work on problem based projects like Blind Navigation Stick, Wheelchair Fallen Detection, Home Automation, Firefighting Robot and many others. Besides these, we had worked on various recreational projects like Maze Runner, Virtual Orchestra, Masterpiece, Hit The Lights and Wireless Lamps.

Niramayam

Who Are We?

Niramayam is a student group of Yoga enthusiasts formed in February 2018 with the objective of spreading awareness among the students regarding the importance of physical as well as mental health, providing complete knowledge on how to be fit and healthy in today's hectic life. Regular yoga practice creates mental clarity and calmness, increases body awareness, relieves chronic stress patterns; relaxes the mind, centers attention, and sharpens concentration which is the most essential part of a student's life. Our aim is to make people self-aware and self-motivated towards their body and health. For this, we organise practice sessions open to all and practise regularly in the college premises.

Our Work

Speading awareness regarding health and fitness, the importance of self-awareness which is essential to live a healthy and successful life. We practice, perform and train the students so that they can train others spreading a healthy environment. We organize health camps, celebrate International Yoga Day, motivate others to practice the same. Our team won a gold medal in Intercollege and secured a good position in All India Inter-University. We perform, we build various formations on energetic songs in college events to energize and motivate students to think about themselves.

Vivdha

Who Are We?

In today's world everyone is living under the arms of demons of stress, depression, monotony and anxiety. Our brains are clamped in the chains of only work and our heart has forgotten to fly. People have become so engrossed in their studies and work-life that they have forgotten to embrace small beauties of the world like the warmth of sunshine, the melodious chirping of birds, changing colors of leaves in different seasons and the coldness of moon. Everyone is so eager to win the ratrace that they choke off their hobbies and interests and hidden talents remain hidden. The only solution to make people feel alive once again is to trigger their imagination by art and one of the most effective ways is storytelling.

Our Work

Vividha-the dramatics makes this possible by following our motto 'bringing life on stage'. Our stories portray the various aspects of the lives of people and bring a poetic smile on their faces. Our humble attempt is to always make people forget about the ratrace and make them just sit back and dive deep into the world of stories. Our wide range of subjects of stories helps to trigger every part of the spectrum of emotions. This club is an amalgamation of colorful actors and wonderfull writers who help to make the lives of audiences beautiful. We try to kill two birds with one stone by breaking the monotony in the lives of our performers as well as our audiences.

Srijan Quest Never ends

Who Are We?

Srijan is the only fine-arts society of JC Bose University, YMCA. The club was established in year 2003 and has maintained its integrity over the years. To select newbies the club organizes three day auditions every year in the month of September or October, followed by a pi round, clearing which will earn you the membership of the Srijan club. Srijan is the family of all its members and everyone is given equal importance in the club. The faculty coordinator and mentor of the club is Dr. Preeti Sethi and all the club activities are under her guidance and supervision. The club activities are headed by the Secrataries and joint secretaries of the club and coordinated by the club members.

Our Work

The club does the work of decorating the main campus of the university on all major events like Elements Culmyca, the annual cultural fest of the university and Reizo the freshers party of the university. Moreover there are numerous artworks made by the club students which are displayed as an art exhibition- Antarang on all events. Also all the graffitis in the university campus are made by the club students. With thier hardwork the club students bring laurels to the college by winning awards at various fest of other colleges and universities. IIT Delhi is one of the examples of the victories of the Srijanites where every year the students of the club win various positions in different competitions.

VIVEKANAND MANCH

Who Are We?

‘VIVEKANAND MANCH’, is the social and cultural club of J.C. Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA, Faridabad. It strives to sensitise the future engineers, scientists, technocrats, entrepreneurs and business leaders from the institute towards their social responsibilities as good human beings. The Vivekanand Manch attempts to bring out momentous changes in the society through interactions at the grassroots level with regards to aspects including education, environmental care, personality development and many more. With the motto, ‘MAN MAKING NATION BUILDING’, the club strives to change small things in the mankind with development of a simple individual to play a bigger role in building of an ideal nation in the future. Vivekanand Manch shares the social responsibility with JCBUST, YMCA, Faridabad, and further invites everyone to participate in all of its events and activities.

Our Work

Vivekanand Manch has contributed to the society with its numerous activities aimed at changing itself and things around. For many years till now and ahead it has been arranging for teaching poor and needy children in the college campus and in adjoining slums. We have made an effort to install multiple pots of water for the birds in campus to ensure water to everyone is not limited just to humans. We often visit nearby old age homes and orphanages to spread smiles in the society. We launch several campaigns for cleanliness, pollution control, water saving etc. in the university premises and hostels.We work hard to add strength to our institute by empowering personalities we have.
