We make people genuinely happy

University Clutural And technical Fest

Elements Culmyca, the annual inter-college cul-tech festival of JC Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA has grown on to become one of the most popular college festivals of NCR with 1000+ participating students, 40+ participating colleges and an expected footfall of over 10000 which tends to increase each year.

Protecting trees with Tree Gaurds

Our University believes in Green Campus. DSW has taken a major step towards it by allotting each club a plant. The members of the club have planted their particular plant in the name of their club. Different plant for every club planted in a queue looks beautiful and nature friendly.

Introducing Students to College Life

Induction is a 20 to 30 days continuous event which takes place during the arrival of a new batch so as to introduce them to the University and its clubs. Induction programme gives an opportunity to the newbies to explore the University campus, get to know about the clubs prevailing in the University and also motivates the students by calling renowned public speakers from all over the world.

Fun Night For New Students

Reizo is the name given to the fresher's of JC Bose University, YMCA. It is a small introduction to the clubs of the University as each club presents their performances like dancing, singing, yoga, anchoring and much more. After the fresher's all the clubs start taking their auditions and let the newbies explore their areas of interest and giving them the chance to be a part of the talented groups.

Manan A Techno Surge

What We Do?

Manan is the computer society of J.C Bose University. We aim to garner creativity and talent & propagate awareness regarding various tech-related knowledge throughout the ingenious minds. We help students realize their potential by providing them a platform where they could show their skills and also a platform for learning and increasing their technical skills icluding coding, development, gaming and much more.
